How they fight

The ally system

Think iron golem meets tamed wolf. Every character runs the same friendly-ally behavior — no taming required in v1, they're on your team from the moment they spawn.

  • Neutral to you. They never target or damage the player.
  • Hunt the monster family. They seek out and melee hostile mobs nearby.
  • Follow the player. They tag along within ~16 blocks so they're there when it matters.
  • Tuned roles. Caine is tanky, Bubble is fast-but-fragile, the rest sit mid-range.

Coming in v2: optional taming (feed a themed item to claim your own follower) and a teleport-to-arena "circus" structure.

Get playing

Install on iPad in four taps

  1. 1

    Get the file

    Grab AmazingDigitalCircus.mcaddon and send it to your iPad via the Files app, AirDrop, or email.

  2. 2

    Tap to import

    Tap the .mcaddon file. Minecraft opens automatically and imports both packs.

  3. 3

    Enable the packs

    When you create or edit a world, turn on both the behavior pack and the resource pack.

  4. 4

    Spawn the cast

    Use the spawn eggs to drop your circus crew into the world — and watch them go to work.
